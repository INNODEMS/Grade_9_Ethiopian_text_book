<subsection xml:id="subsec-Area-of-a-rhombus">
  <title>Area of a rhombus</title>
  <p>
    A rhombus is a type of quadrilateral that is a special kind of parallelogram. The key characteristics of a rhombus are:
  </p>
  <p>
    <ol>
      <li>
        <p>
          Opposite sides are parallel.
        </p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>
          All sides have equal length.
        </p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>
          Opposite angles are equal, and adjacent angles are supplementary (they add up to 180°).
        </p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>
          The diagonals of a rhombus bisect each other at right angles (90°), meaning they intersect at 
          a right angle and divide each other into two equal parts.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ol>
  </p>
  <p>
    The formula for the area <m>A</m> of a rhombus can be given in two common ways:
  </p>
  <p>
    <ul>
      <li>
        <p>
          Using the diagonals: <me>A=\frac{1}{2}\times d_1 \times d_2</me>
        </p>
        <p>
          where:
        </p>
        <p>
          <m>d_1 </m>and <m>d_2</m> are the lengths of the two diagonals.
        </p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>
          Using the base and height: <me>A= \text{base} \times \text{height}</me>
        </p>
        <p>
          Where the base is the length of any side (since all sides are equal) and the 
          height is the perpendicular distance between opposite sides.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </p>
  <activity>
    <statement>
      <p>
        Calculate the area of the figure given it's 
        diagonals are <m>6cm</m> and <m>8cm</m>.
      </p>
      <image source="/Rhombus.png" width="40%">
        <shortdescription>Rhombus</shortdescription>
      </image>
    </statement>
    <solution>
      <p>
        Area of a rhombus <m>= \frac{1}{2}</m> short diagonals <m>\times</m> long diagonal.
      </p>
      <p>
        <md>
          <mrow> A=\amp \frac{1}{2}d_1 d_2</mrow>
          <mrow> A=\amp \frac{1}{2} (6 \times 8)cm^2 </mrow>
          <mrow> A=\amp 24 cm^2 </mrow>
        </md>
      </p>
    </solution>
  </activity>
