  <title>Area of a parallelogram</title>
  <p>
    <ul>
      <li>
        <p>
          Rectangle is a type of parallelogram where all the angles are 90Â°
        </p>
      </li>
      <li>
        <p>
          All rectangles are parallelograms, but not all parallelograms are rectangles.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </p>
  <p>
    <md>
      <mrow>\text{Area of a parallelogram} = \amp \text{base} \times \text{height}</mrow>
      <mrow>= \amp bh </mrow>
    </md>
  </p>
  <p>
    The figure below shows a parallelogram with base and height labeled. 
  </p>

  <activity>
    <statement>
      <p>
        Muchemi's piece of land is in the shape below.
      </p>
      <image source="/parallelogram.png" width="40%">
        <shortdescription>Parallelogram</shortdescription>
      </image>
      <p>
        What is the area of the piece of land?
      </p>
    </statement>
    <solution>
      <p>
        <md>
          <mrow> \text{Area of a parallelogram}=\amp \text{base length} \times \text{Perpendicular height}</mrow>
          <mrow> =\amp 120m \times 40m</mrow>
          <mrow> =\amp 4800m^2 </mrow>
        </md>
      </p>
      <p>
        The area of the piece of land is <m>4800m^2</m>.
      </p>
    </solution>
    
  </activity>

    NOTE:
  </p>
