          <li>
            <p>
              The figure formed between the tree, ladder 
              and the ground is a right-angled tringle.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              A right-angle triangle has three sides: 
              the <em>base</em>, the <em>perpendicular height</em> 
              and the <em>hypotenuse</em>.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              The side that is <m>8 m</m> long is the <em>perpendicular height</em>.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              The side that is <m>6 m</m> long is the <em>base</em>.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              The side that is <m>10 m</m> long is the <em>hypotenuse</em>. 
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              The longest side in a right-angled triangle is 
              the <em>hypotenuse</em>.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              The two shorter sides of this figure are the 
              <em>base</em> and the <em>perpendicular height</em>.
            </p>

        </ul>
