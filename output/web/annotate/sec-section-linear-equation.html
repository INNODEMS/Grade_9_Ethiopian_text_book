        <statement>
            <p>
                Solve the following simultanios equation using the elimination method:
            </p>
            <p>
                <md>
                    <mrow> 4x-2y=\amp 7</mrow>
                    <mrow> 2x+y=\amp 7</mrow>
                </md>
            </p>
        </statement>
        <hint>
            <p>
                <ul>
                    <li>
                        <p>
                            <em>Multiply each equation by a suitable number to make the multiplying
                            factor to one unknown equal in both equation.</em>
                        </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                        <p>
                            <em>Eliminate one unknown by either subtracting or adding
                            the two equations formed</em>
                        </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                        <p>
                            <em>Solve for the remaining unknown.</em>
                        </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                        <p>
                            <em>Use the soluition to solve the other unknown.</em>
                        </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                        <p>
                            <em>The ubove is the elimination method of
                             solving simultaneous equation</em>
                        </p>
                    </li>
                </ul>
            </p>
        </hint>
        <solution>
            <p>
               <md>
                <mrow> 3x-2y=\amp 7.........(i)</mrow>
                <mrow> 2x+y=\amp 7 .........(ii)</mrow>
               </md> 
            </p>
            <p>
              Multiply equation (ii) by 2 to get:
            </p>
            <p>
                <m>4x+2y=14........(iii)</m>
            </p>
            <p>
                <md>
                    <mrow> 3x-2y=\amp 7.......(i)</mrow>
                    <mrow> +4x+2y=\amp14........(iii) \text{add(i) to (iii)} </mrow>
                    <mrow> 7x=\amp 21</mrow>
                    <mrow> x=\amp 3</mrow>
                </md>
            </p>
            <p>
                substitute <m>x=3</m> in <m>3x-2y=7</m>
            </p>
            <p>
                <md>
                    <mrow>3(3)-2y= \amp 7</mrow>
                    <mrow> 9-2y=\amp 7</mrow>
                    <mrow> 9-7=\amp 2y</mrow>
                    <mrow> 2=\amp 2y </mrow>
                    <mrow> 1=\amp y </mrow>
                    <mrow> \text{Therefore},x=\amp3 \amp  and  \amp y=\amp1 </mrow>
                </md>
              
                <men xml:id="eqn-soluition">  x=3 \text{and} y=1
                    
                </men>
                Our soluition is in <xref ref="eqn-soluition" />
                

            </p>
            
        </solution>
