    <introduction>
      <p>
        Distance is the length of the space between two points. eg
      </p>
      <p>
        Distance from the  grade 7 classroom and the assembly, home to school, assembly to your school latrine and etc.
      </p>
      <p>
        The SI unit of measuring distance is <m>kilometres \, \text{and} \, metres</m>.
      </p>
      <p>

            <p>
              Kilometres can be written as <m>km</m>.
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
            <p>
              Metres can also be written <m>m</m>.

            </p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </p>
