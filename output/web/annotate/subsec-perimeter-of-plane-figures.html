<subsection xml:id="subsec-perimeter-of-plane-figures">
  <title>Perimeter of plane figures</title>
  <activity>
    <statement>
      <p>
        We need paper cut-outs and a ruler.
      </p>
      <p>
      <ol>
        <li>
          <p>
            Make paper cut-outs of different plane figures.
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
           Make paper cut-outs of different plane combined shapes. 
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
            Measure the distance round each shape using a piece of string and a ruler.
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
            Compare the answers in step 2 and 3 and note the difference.
          </p>
        </li>
      </ol>
      </p>
      <p>
        Perimeter is the distance round a plane figure. To work out perimeter, you add the lengths of the sides of a given plane figure.
      </p>
    </statement>
  </activity>
  <example>

    <solution>
      <p>
        <ol cols="2" marker="a)">
          <li>
            <p> Perimeter of a square.
              <md>
                <mrow>=\amp l+l+l+l </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp 4l</mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp 4 \times 10cm </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp  \textbf {40cm} </mrow>
              </md>
            </p>
          </li>
          <li>
              <p> Perimeter of a rectangle.
              <md>
                <mrow>=\amp l+w+l+w </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp 2 \times (l + w) </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp 2 \times (40cm +8cm) </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp 2 \times 48cm </mrow>
                <mrow>=\amp  \textbf {96cm} </mrow>

              </md>
            </p>
          </li>
        </ol>
      </p>
    </solution>
  </example>

    <solution>
      <p>
        We add all the sides to get the perimeter.
        <md>
          <mrow>\text{perimeter}=\amp4\text {cm} + 3 \text {cm} + 7\text {cm }+ 7\text {cm} + 3\text {cm} + 4\text {cm} + 15\text {cm}</mrow>
          <mrow>=\amp \textbf {43cm} </mrow>
        </md>
      </p>
    </solution>
