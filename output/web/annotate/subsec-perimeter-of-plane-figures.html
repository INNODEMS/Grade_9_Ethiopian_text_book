<subsection xml:id="subsec-perimeter-of-plane-figures">
  <title>Perimeter of plane figures</title>
  <activity>
    <statement>
      <p>
        We need paper cut-outs and a ruler.
      </p>
      <ol>
        <li>
          <p>
            Make paper cut-outs of different plane figures.
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
           Make paper cut-outs of different plane combined shapes. 
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
            Measure the distance round each shape using a piece of string and a ruler.
          </p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <p>
            Compare the answers in step 2 and 3 and note the difference.
          </p>
        </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
        Perimeter is the distance round a plane figure. To work out perimeter, you add the lengths of the sides of a given plane figure.
      </p>
    </statement>
  </activity>
